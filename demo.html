The Problem with Current DAO Governance

Why only 10% of DAO members participate in voting

01

Public Voting = Whale Dominance

Large token holders can see exactly how everyone voted and apply pressure. Small holders are coerced into voting along with whales or choosing not to vote at all.

02

Voter Intimidation

When your voting record is permanently etched on the blockchain, you risk retaliation, community backlash, or being blacklisted by projects you oppose.

03

Prohibitive Gas Fees

On Ethereum mainnet, a single vote costs $50-100. This excludes ordinary participants, resulting in only 10% voting while the top 10% control 80% of power.
