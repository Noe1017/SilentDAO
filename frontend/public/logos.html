üéØ Design Philosophy

The SilentDAO logo combines three powerful symbols: a shield for protection, an eye for transparency, and a checkmark for voting. Together, they represent our mission to enable private, verifiable, and secure governance on the Mantle network. The animated pupil and floating effect bring life to the brand, while the clean, modern aesthetic appeals to both technical and non-technical users.
